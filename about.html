In this we application, you can book domestic flights. You can register and create an account. After registration you can login which will redirect you to booking page. In booking page you can fill out the form with information about boarding point,destination,time and date and book the flight. The main feature of this web appplication is that, if there is no direct route between boarding point and destination then our algorithm will show shortest available path . After confirming the booking, you will be redirected to payment page where you can play online with debit/credit card. All the bank details will be encrypted and stored in database for safety purposes.
